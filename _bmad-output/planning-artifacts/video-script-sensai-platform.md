# SensAI Platform — Instructional Video Script (v2)

**Format:** Screenshot slides + AI voiceover (ElevenLabs)
**Estimated Duration:** 10–12 minutes
**Tone:** Cinematic storytelling. Confident. Authoritative. Human. SensAI as visionaries who see what others can't.

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Visual:** Dark screen. Text fades in one line at a time, timed to narration. No logo yet — just words and voice.

**Script:**

> A patient sits at home. It's late. They've just been told they need to see a specialist.
>
> They open their laptop. They search. They find a name on a hospital website.
>
> No photo. A two-line biography. No qualifications listed. No idea if this person even takes their insurance.
>
> They close the tab. They look somewhere else.
>
> That moment — that single moment of lost trust — is invisible. No one at the hospital knows it happened. No alert fired. No report was generated. The patient simply... disappeared.
>
> But what if you could see it?

---

## SCENE 2 — The Scale of the Invisible Problem
**Duration:** ~45 seconds
**Visual:** Numbers animate onto screen: "3,800 profiles." "31 hospitals." "Hundreds of specialties." Then a screenshot of a real Nuffield profile page — one with gaps visible. Annotations highlight: empty photo, thin bio, no pricing.

**Script:**

> Nuffield Health is the UK's largest healthcare charity. Over three thousand eight hundred consultant profiles live on their website right now. Every single one of them is a first impression. A moment of trust — or doubt.
>
> And here's what nobody had ever measured before: how many of those profiles are actually working? How many have a photo? A biography that a patient can understand? Qualifications that build confidence? A booking button that leads somewhere real?
>
> The answer was uncomfortable. Hundreds of profiles were incomplete. Some were almost empty. And the ones that looked fine on the surface? Many were filled with medical jargon that no patient could follow.
>
> The problem wasn't negligence. It was invisibility. Nobody had the tools to see it at scale.
>
> Until now.

---

## SCENE 3 — Enter SensAI
**Duration:** ~35 seconds
**Visual:** SensAI logo fades in — the eye icon, teal on dark. Tagline: "Unifying Vision with AI." Beat. Then: "Working in partnership with Nuffield Health." The sidebar navigation of the platform slides in from the right, revealing the full application.

**Script:**

> SensAI was built for exactly this kind of problem — the kind that hides in plain sight.
>
> We specialise in AI-powered business intelligence. We find the patterns that humans can't see at scale. We measure what was never measured before. And then we do something that most analytics platforms don't: we provide the tools to fix it.
>
> Not just insight. Action. Not just a report. A transformation engine.
>
> What you're about to see is the platform we built in partnership with Nuffield Health — and it changes the way a healthcare organisation understands its own consultant network.

---

## SCENE 4 — The Dashboard: Seeing Everything at Once
**Duration:** ~70 seconds
**Visual:** Full dashboard screenshot. Camera slowly pans across KPI cards (zoom each one briefly). Then tier donut chart fills frame. Then quick actions panel. Then hospital leaderboard. Each section gets 10-15 seconds of focus.

**Script:**

> The moment you log in, the Executive Dashboard tells you the truth about your network — in seconds.
>
> Eight key metrics, each one a story. Total profiles analysed. Average quality score out of a hundred. The percentage that have earned Gold tier status — the standard you want every consultant to reach. How many are bookable online. How many have been flagged for urgent review.
>
> And this one matters more than people realise — the average Plain English score. Because a biography stuffed with medical terminology might impress a peer, but it terrifies a patient. We measure readability, and we score it.
>
> Now look at the tier distribution. Gold. Silver. Bronze. Incomplete. This single chart is the most honest snapshot of your consultant network you've ever had. And every segment is clickable — tap Gold and you see exactly who's there. Tap Incomplete and you see who needs help.
>
> Below that, the Quick Actions panel. This is where the platform stops being passive. It's identified the six highest-impact improvements you could make right now — ranked by total network uplift. "Add profile photos — 847 profiles affected — 10 points each." That's not a suggestion. That's a strategy.
>
> And the Hospital Leaderboard? Every hospital ranked by average score, with tier distribution bars showing the quality mix. You can see in an instant which hospitals are leading — and which ones are falling behind.

---

## SCENE 5 — Consultant Explorer: Finding Anyone, Anything
**Duration:** ~55 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Consultants page — filter sidebar visible, table populated. Second screenshot: filters applied (e.g., Bronze tier + no photo + Cardiology), showing a narrowed result set with filter chips visible.

**Script:**

> Now you need to go deeper. You've seen the big picture — but you need to find specific consultants, answer specific questions.
>
> The Consultant Explorer puts over ten dimensions of filtering at your fingertips. Quality tier. Booking state. Hospital. Specialty. Biography depth. Photo status. Severity flags. Content gaps. Score range. Combine them however you want.
>
> "Show me every Bronze-tier cardiologist across London hospitals who doesn't have a profile photo." Three clicks. Done. The answer appears instantly.
>
> Every column in the results table is sortable. Every row is a doorway to that consultant's full story. And here's where it gets powerful: select ten consultants with the checkboxes, hit "Batch Rewrite," and the AI improvement engine starts working on all of them simultaneously.
>
> Need to share what you've found? The Export button generates a CSV of exactly what you're looking at — filtered, sorted, ready for a stakeholder email. Contact data is excluded by default, because data governance isn't optional.

---

## SCENE 6 — Consultant Profile: The Full Story
**Duration:** ~60 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of a consultant detail page — score gauge prominent, tier badge visible, flags shown. Second screenshot: AI Assessment tab open showing evidence text.

**Script:**

> Click a name, and you see everything we know about that consultant's online presence.
>
> The score gauge is the centrepiece — a single number out of a hundred, colour-coded by tier. But it's not just a number. Right beside it, you can see how this consultant compares to the average in their specialty. Are they above or below? By how much?
>
> Quality flags appear as severity badges. Red means something is critically wrong — a missing biography, a profile that might not even be a Nuffield consultant. Amber is a warning. Blue is informational.
>
> Four tabs break down the detail. Overview gives you qualifications, memberships, languages. Content Quality assesses the biography — how deep is it? Is it readable? Are treatments listed with specificity? Booking shows real availability — not just "bookable in theory" but "has actual appointment slots in the next 28 days."
>
> And then there's the AI Assessment tab. This is where SensAI is different from every other analytics tool. We don't just give you a score — we explain it. The AI tells you precisely why this biography was rated "thin." Why the plain English score is a three. What would need to change to reach Gold.
>
> Every decision, transparent. Every score, justified. No black boxes.

---

## SCENE 7 — Hospital Benchmarking: Who's Leading, Who's Not
**Duration:** ~35 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Hospitals page — card grid with tier distribution bars, scores, metrics.

**Script:**

> When you manage a network of hospitals, you need to know who's setting the standard — and who needs support.
>
> The Hospital Benchmarking page lays it bare. Every hospital, presented as a card with its average quality score, consultant count, booking rates, and a tier distribution bar that tells the story at a glance.
>
> One hospital is eighty percent Gold. Another is sixty percent Incomplete. The difference is visible in a heartbeat.
>
> Click any card and you drill straight into that hospital's consultants. The Hospital Comparison tool lets you put two side by side — so when one hospital is outperforming, you can see exactly what they're doing differently and share it across the network.

---

## SCENE 8 — Specialty Analysis: Different Standards, Same Rigour
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Specialties page — cards with metrics, heatmap visible.

**Script:**

> Not all specialties are the same. An orthopaedic surgeon's profile has different expectations to a dermatologist's. The platform understands this.
>
> The Specialty Analysis page breaks quality down by medical discipline. Each specialty card shows average score, tier distribution, photo rates, booking rates, and common quality flags.
>
> The heatmap gives you a bird's-eye view across every specialty at once — hot spots and cold spots, instantly visible. And the outlier detection flags anomalies: a specialty where photo rates are thirty percent below the network average, or where booking is disproportionately low. These are the patterns that would take a human analyst weeks to find.

---

## SCENE 9 — Action Centre: From Insight to Impact
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Actions page — impact summary cards at top (current vs projected, with green deltas), prioritised actions table below.

**Script:**

> This is the page that changes the conversation. Every other page shows you where you are. The Action Centre shows you where you could be.
>
> Four impact cards at the top. Current average score: 62.3. Projected score if every recommended action is completed: 71.8. That's a 9.5-point network-wide improvement. Current Gold percentage: 28%. Projected: 41%. Thirteen percentage points of consultants elevated to the highest quality standard.
>
> These aren't hypothetical numbers. They're calculated from the actual data — profile by profile, point by point.
>
> Below, every improvement opportunity is ranked by total impact. "Add profile photos" — 847 profiles, 10 points each, 8,470 points of total uplift. "Write missing biographies" — 312 profiles, 15 points each. "Add qualifications." "List accepted insurers."
>
> Click any action and you're taken directly to the affected profiles. Export them. Review them. Send them to the Rewrite Engine. The path from "here's the problem" to "here's it being fixed" is measured in clicks, not weeks.

---

## SCENE 10 — Review Queue: The Human Safety Net
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Review page — flagged profiles with severity badges, checkboxes, action buttons.

**Script:**

> AI is powerful, but not everything should be automated. Some decisions need a human eye.
>
> The Review Queue catches what needs attention. Profiles stuck at Incomplete tier. Profiles with critical fail-severity flags — potential CMS corruption, non-Nuffield hospitals appearing in the data, low-confidence extractions where the AI isn't sure it read the page correctly.
>
> Each flagged profile shows exactly why it's here, with severity badges and direct links to the detail page or the Rewrite Engine. Mark it reviewed, take action, move on. Nothing gets lost. Nothing slips through.

---

## SCENE 11 — Reports: Stakeholder-Ready in Seconds
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Reports page — PDF preview tab showing a professional executive report. Second screenshot: CSV export builder with field selection.

**Script:**

> When it's time to present to leadership, the Reports section does the heavy lifting.
>
> The PDF Report is a complete executive document — title page, AI-generated summary, KPI snapshot, tier breakdown, top performers, at-risk profiles, hospital leaderboard. Professional. Polished. Ready to send without a single edit.
>
> The CSV Export Builder gives you granular control over data exports — choose your fields, apply your filters, download. Contact data excluded by default, toggled on only when authorised.
>
> Run History tracks every data collection with timestamps, success rates, and duration — a full audit trail for governance and compliance.

---

## SCENE 12 — The Rewrite Engine: Where AI Becomes Action
**Duration:** ~70 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Rewrite page — full workspace visible. Element cards showing "Biography" with current content. Second screenshot: after rewrite, showing before/after comparison, source evidence panel with web citations, projected score delta. Third screenshot (if possible): benchmark bar showing top performers.

**Script:**

> Everything you've seen so far has been about understanding the problem. The Rewrite Engine is about solving it.
>
> This is SensAI's most advanced capability — and there is nothing else like it.
>
> Select a consultant. The platform breaks their profile into elements: biography, treatments, qualifications, specialty details, memberships, clinical interests. Each element shows the current content and the maximum points available for improvement.
>
> Hit "Rewrite" and watch what happens. The system launches a multi-stage research pipeline. It searches the web for verified information about this specific consultant — published papers, professional body listings, hospital biographies, media coverage. It fetches those sources. It extracts facts. And then — crucially — it cross-checks every fact across multiple sources. Corroboration. Because AI-generated content that's wrong is worse than no content at all.
>
> Only after verification does the AI generate improved content. The result appears side by side — original on the left, improved on the right. Every claim is linked to its source. You can see exactly where each fact came from.
>
> And the platform shows you the projected impact: "This rewrite adds 14 points to the consultant's score, moving them from Bronze to Silver."
>
> Accept it. Reject it. The control is yours.
>
> For scale, batch processing lets you queue dozens of profiles and let the engine work through them one by one. And the Benchmark Bar shows what the best profiles in the network look like — so you're not just improving, you're aiming for a standard that already exists in your own data.

---

## SCENE 13 — Configuration: Your Rules, Your Standards
**Duration:** ~35 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot of Configuration page — editable weights table, tier thresholds, gate rules, flag definitions.

**Script:**

> The quality framework isn't fixed. It's yours to define.
>
> The Configuration page gives you full control over every scoring weight, every tier threshold, and every gate rule. If photos matter more than memberships, increase the weight. If Gold should require a score of 85 instead of 80, change it. If you want to block Gold status for any consultant with a fail-severity flag, toggle that rule on.
>
> Thirteen quality flags, each with a defined severity level. Mandatory gate requirements that go beyond the numerical score — because a consultant with 82 points but no photo shouldn't be Gold.
>
> Every change takes effect on the next data run. The platform adapts to your evolving standards, not the other way around.

---

## SCENE 14 — AI Copilot: Ask Anything
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Visual:** Screenshot showing the Copilot widget open, overlaying the dashboard. A natural language query visible: "Show me all Bronze cardiologists in London without photos." Response visible with a link.

**Script:**

> One more thing. Available from anywhere in the platform — the AI Copilot.
>
> Press Control-K. A search bar appears. Type a question in plain English. "Which hospital has the lowest average score?" "How many orthopaedic surgeons are Gold tier?" "Show me consultants who are bookable but have no available slots."
>
> The Copilot understands the question, queries the full dataset, and gives you an answer — or a direct link to the filtered results. It's like having a senior data analyst sitting beside you, twenty-four hours a day.

> Let's step back and see what this partnership has made possible.

> Before SensAI, Nuffield Health had nearly four thousand consultant profiles — and no scalable way to measure their quality. No way to benchmark hospitals against each other. No way to know which improvements would matter most. No way to fix profiles at scale without months of manual work.
>
> Now, every profile is scored. Every gap is quantified. Every improvement is ranked by impact. And an AI engine can generate evidence-backed content improvements faster than any human team.
>
> That patient sitting at home, searching for a specialist? They deserve a profile that builds confidence. A photo. A clear biography. Real qualifications. Available appointments.
>
> This is what SensAI delivers. Not just data. Not just dashboards. A measurable transformation in how an organisation presents its most important people.

### ElevenLabs Voice Settings
- **Voice:** British English, confident — recommend "Daniel" or "Charlotte" on ElevenLabs
- **Style:** Think BBC documentary narrator. Measured but warm. Authoritative but human.
- **Speed:** Slightly slower than conversational — let key phrases land
- **Emphasis points:** Mark with [pause] in the text when generating audio
- **Total text:** ~2,800 words ≈ approx 14,000 characters (may need ElevenLabs Starter plan at $5/mo for the full script, or split across two months on free tier)

### Emotional Arc
```
Hook (vulnerability, empathy)
  → Problem (scale, urgency, stakes)
    → SensAI enters (confidence, expertise, authority)
      → Platform walkthrough (power, precision, control)
        → Rewrite Engine (wonder, capability, "nothing else like it")
          → Transformation (pride, impact, measurable change)
            → Close (trust, invitation)
```
